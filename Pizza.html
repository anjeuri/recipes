Best Pizza Recipe

Description:

This is the best pizza recipe ever that was taught to me by my grandmother back in 2012. I have made hundreds of pizzas maybe thousands to this day and they always taste perfect.

Ingredient List:

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • pepperoni
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Knead our dough.
  2. Add our tomato sauce to our circular dough.
  3. Add cheese on top of our tomato sauce.
  4. Spread pepperoni onto the pizza at your own liking.
  5. Put the pizza in the oven at 350F for 30 minutes.
  6. Let the pizza cool down and enjoy!
